NUCLEAR POWER

Why am I seeing this ad?

A growing concern has been placed on relieving the effects of Global Warming, primarily by today's youth. As the people who will live long enough to see the world burn, many are fighting for ways to alleviate the problem, such as finding cleaner sources of energy. This aspect in particular can be answered relatively efficiently. A switch to nuclear power in the near future would be a great stepping stone. Not only is it extremely efficient at producing energy, the process that allows nuclear energy to be converted to electricity is clean of pollution. Due to it's availability, we suggest that using it in the near future as a placeholder until renewables become more efficient or more easily accessable is a good way of fighting climate change.
